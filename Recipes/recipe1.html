Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat griddle or skillet to medium-high heat. Lightly grease griddle.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mix together the flout, sugar, salt, and baking powder.
  3. In a separate bowl whisk together the egg, milk, butter, and vanilla.
  4. Add the flour mixture into the wet mixture. Gently mix together until combined but do not over stir, some lumps are completely normal.
  5. Using a 1/4 cup measuring cup measure out the pancake batter and add to griddle, spreading slightly if needed gently into a circle. Continue scooping the batter until the griddle/skillet is full.
  6. Cook pancakes about 2-3 minutes on each side,or when bubbles start to pop in the center of each pancake. Repeat until batter is completely used. Serve immediately.
